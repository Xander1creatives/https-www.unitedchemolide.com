About Us

Our company provides systems for the distribution of clean water and waste water. United Chemolide manufactures and assembles specialised KSB pumps for chemical mine waste disposal, tailings disposal and sewage, amongst other things. It uses Siemens automation systems for power transmission and control. United Chemolide began its fluid processing installation service in 1986 in response to numerous requests for water rehabilitation and reticulation projects by District Councils. Supply of both Domestic and Industrial Pumps, Solar products, irrigation equipment, electrical components, Water Treatment Solutions and construction services. We stock high quality products to suit your need.
